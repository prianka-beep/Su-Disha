About Us

Welcome to Sudisha Foundation

We are the foundation with a aim to spread awareness about menstrual hygiene and related problems. Our vision is to help underprivileged girls and women by providing them sanitary pads/napkins. We believe that together we will change the mindset of society about menstrual talk which is no more a #Taboo thing. We are registered under Madhya Pradesh Society Registration act 1973 having registration no. 05/22/03/15948/21.

Our motto is :
"To serve women, and to ease their menstrual pain by educating them about menstrual hygiene".
1. To spread awareness about menstrual hygiene and diseases caused due to unhygienic routine.
2. To distribute free sanitary napkins and spread awareness about it to all the needy women who are not even aware of it.
3. To make sure they can reach out to us if they need any help.
4. Gifting them with all, what they need during the days of period as well if really necessary, then fulfilling their essential needs also.
Distribution of sanitary napkins.
5. To educate and aware people about Yoga, meditation and other mental exercises.
