Mission :

To enhance literacy rate regarding menstrual hygiene among each women. And to ensure that every needy woman is within reach of clean and hygienic sanitary napkins for her toughest days.



Vision :

Our vision is to aid and reach out every needy hand with sanitary pads and to spread awareness about same in the society. To make the society aware about the severity, hygiene and solution of menstruation.
